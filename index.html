A Quick Note About Us

We Specialize in Creating Everlasting Memories

Who Are WE


Perfect Endings Events is a full-service event planning, design, and coordination company. We have been creating Perfect Endings for our clients over the last 15 years for Weddings, Corporate Events, and just those special occasions. We specialize in creating tailored events that leaving lasting impressions for our clients in Greater St. Louis and the surrounding areas.

The firm provide configurable packages, and its services include budget creation and management, vendor tours, accommodation and oversight of the event proper. The firm also handles post-event tasks like venue break-down and the return of rented items.

Our Vision

Planning a Major Event can be an exciting and wonderful experience. It can also be overwhelmingly stressful but you've taken the first step in executing a fabulous day...by visiting us! Our goal is for our Couples to fully enjoy their engagement and have a great experience the months, weeks and days leading up to one of the biggest events of your lives!

During our complimentary planning session, we will listen intently to what you've planned to date and provide a custom planning package that represents the services that you actually need. We do offer a select number of Month of Planning Services per year which are only booked within 6 months of your wedding date. OneTouch Events is committed to working with each Couple to tailor an experience that is specific to their needs.
